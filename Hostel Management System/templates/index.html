About

The hostel is located in the south-end of the campus where the accommodation is provided to about 300 students. No rangging is encouraged in the hostel. Hostel is well secured with security staff and the CCTV services. Cleanliness is also well mentained.

  • TV room
  • Reading room
  • R.O Drinking Water-1000 L/Hr
  • 24 X 7 Electricity
  • Hot Water Facility
